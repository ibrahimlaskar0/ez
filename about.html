About Esplendidez 2026

The premier tech fest of Regional Institute of Science and Technology, bringing together innovation, competition, and cultural celebration.

Our Vision

Esplendidez 2026 is the annual techno-cultural fest of the Regional Institute of Science and Technology (RIST), Meghalaya, affiliated with NEHU. It’s a vibrant celebration of innovation, creativity, and culture, bringing together students from across Northeast India.

The fest blends technical brilliance and artistic expression, featuring hackathons, project expos, workshops, cultural nights, and sports events. It serves as a platform for students to innovate, compete, and collaborate, reflecting RIST’s vision of nurturing both technical excellence and creative talent.

With modern infrastructure and an enthusiastic student community, Esplendidez has been a proud tradition for over a decade, growing into one of Northeast India's most awaited festivals — a true fusion of technology, art, and youthful energy.
